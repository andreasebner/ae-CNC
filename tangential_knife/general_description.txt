I have a CNC Router using UCCNC with a tangential Knife equiped. 

The UCCNC automatically calculates the A Axis movements and befoer each XY movement the z axis is lifeted
and the knife axis adjusted accordingly. 

I use Lightburn as the CAM which in general is fine, but there is a problem with the G0 Z moves. 

Like mentioned UCCNC lifts the z axis before each x y move and adjust the a axis. But this not done for 
moves of the Z axis. This means it execuets the G0 Z movement, than automatically lifts the a axis and then follow
the next G1 step. But the first G0 Z move is done with a non adjusted A knife. 
Therefore wen need to adjust the A axis before the Z movement. The angle must be calculated based on the 
movement which happens after the Z move.
